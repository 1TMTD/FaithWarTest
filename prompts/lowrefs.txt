### Regions (10 Regions)
1.  Light Domain (Svitliy Kray) Majestic white temples, golden sunlight, ethereal architecture, divine atmosphere.
2.  Dark Forest (Temniy Lis) Cursed lands, deep shadows, mystical runes carved on ancient tree bark, eerie ambiance.
3.  Mountain Peaks (Horski Vershyny) Snow-capped summits, magical crystals, harsh climate, solitary hermit figures.
4.  Golden Coast (Zolote Uzberezhzhia) Maritime ports, sun-drenched treasures, luxurious diplomatic residences, sparkling water.
5.  Burning Desert (Palaiucha Pustelia) Fiery sands, ruins of ancient civilizations, massive sandstorms, scorched earth.
6.  Silent Swamps (Tykhy Bolota) Isolated region, sacred marsh flora, thick bluish fog, mysterious atmosphere.
7.  Silver Plains (Sribni Rivnyny) Endless fields of silver wheat, agricultural serenity, bountiful harvests.
8.  Shadow Valley (Tini Dolyny) Mystical spirits, veil of sorrow, play of shadows between jagged rocks.
9.  Forest Depths (Lisovi Hlybyny) Sacred sanctuary forest, healing herbs, feeling of spiritual rebirth.
10. Deep Lakes (Hlyboki Ozera) Island settlements, mystical water festivals, mirrored water surface.
